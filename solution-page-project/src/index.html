IRCS in Action

To test how well IRCS catches mud, we built a Mini Wetland Sediment Trapping System using a clear bottle. By slowing the water flow, dirt particles settled fast, just like what happens behind stick fences.

Testing showed about 75% of the mud stayed put, proving that slower water lets the shore rebuild.

Our test proved the key idea: slow the water, and the land comes back.

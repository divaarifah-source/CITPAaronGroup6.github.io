Design & Selection

This section documents criteria, alternatives, and the decision matrix that led to selecting IRCS.

Mandatory Design Criteria

  • Environmental: promote mangrove regeneration, blue carbon sequestration.
  • Cultural: respect traditional land use and cultural sites.
  • Economic: cost-effective, creates local employment and revenue streams.

Initial Design Options

  1. Rock Revetment
  2. Offshore Reef
  3. Integrated Restorative Coastal System (IRCS)
